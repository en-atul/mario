Dropdown menus provide a list of actions or options that appear when triggered. They close automatically when clicking outside or selecting an item.

<Primary />

<Controls />

## Usage

Pass an array of items with `label` and `onClick` handlers. Use separators to group related items visually.

## Alignment

- **left**: Menu aligns to the left edge of trigger (default)
- **right**: Menu aligns to the right edge of trigger

## Items

Each item can be:
- **enabled**: Clickable with hover state
- **disabled**: Non-interactive, grayed out
- **separator**: Visual divider between groups
