Switches are toggle controls that allow users to turn settings on or off. They provide immediate visual feedback for binary choices.

<Primary />

<Controls />

## Usage

Use switches for settings that take effect immediately, such as notifications or feature toggles. For form submissions, consider using checkboxes instead.

## States

- **checked**: Toggle is on
- **unchecked**: Toggle is off
- **disabled**: Non-interactive state
